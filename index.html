About

The human
behind the queries

I'm a Data Engineer who ended up here because I genuinely can't stop asking "wait, why does this take so long?" and "who actually uses this report?" I've spent the last few years as a contractor at Bourntec Solutions, embedded across clients in public health, insurance, energy markets, and enterprise operations — building systems that people actually trust and use.

"I don't just build pipelines. I build pipelines your team will still be thanking you for two years later."

My work lives at that rare intersection of deep technical fluency and the ability to explain it to a room full of people who'd rather be anywhere else. I've navigated undocumented databases with no data dictionary, enabled a public health division to participate in national research for the first time, and once trained a non-technical team so well they started catching their own data anomalies. That last one felt like a small win for humanity.

I hold an M.S. in Data Science from Indiana University, a Microsoft Power BI certification, and a Lean Six Sigma Green Belt — because apparently I enjoy making processes more efficient in my spare time too. I also serve as a peer reviewer for international conferences and a judge for national hackathons, because good ideas deserve sharp feedback.
